# Boondia  

### ingredients  

item| quantity
--|---
besan| 1 cup
water| 3/4 cup
oil(optional)|1tsp


### syrap  

item| quantity 
--|--
sugar|1 cup
water|1/2 cup


### steps

1. prepare dough. 
1. should be fried in hot oil. 
1. no sahking required. 
1. fry for a min or so. 
1. add fried boondi on hot sugar. 
1. wait 1 hour to let it soak the syrap.
